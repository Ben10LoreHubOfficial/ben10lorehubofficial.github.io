Alien

Heatblast

Pyronite • Master of Fire

Overview

Heatblast was the very first alien Ben Tennyson unlocked after finding the Omnitrix, and he remains one of the most iconic forms in the watch's library. His molten rock body and constant flame give him an intimidating presence on any battlefield, and his fiery design made him an instant fan favorite from the start of the series.

Powers & Abilities

Heatblast can generate and hurl fireballs, create walls of flame, and tolerate extreme heat without any harm to himself. His body is largely immune to fire-based attacks, and he can melt through metal and stone with sustained heat. He is, unsurprisingly, vulnerable to extreme cold and large amounts of water, which can weaken or even extinguish his flame.

Background

Pyronites are a fire-based species that naturally live on volcanic, magma-covered worlds. Their bodies are composed of living molten rock, allowing them to survive in environments that would instantly destroy most other life forms. Heatblast's species rarely appears elsewhere in the show, making this form one of the more unique entries in Ben's collection.
