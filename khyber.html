Villain

Khyber

Unknown Species • The Huntsman

Overview

Khyber is a near-silent, highly skilled alien hunter who considers Ben one of the ultimate trophies worth pursuing. His quiet, calculating demeanor makes him one of the more unnerving antagonists Ben encounters.

Powers & Abilities

Khyber is an elite tracker and combatant, skilled with a variety of alien weaponry and traps. He's frequently accompanied by a loyal alien companion that assists him in hunting down targets across different worlds.

Background

Little is revealed about Khyber's origins or motivations beyond his singular passion for the hunt. His patience and precision set him apart from louder, more impulsive villains, making every encounter with him feel like a calculated game.
