Alien

Grey Matter

Galvan • Tiny Genius

Overview

Grey Matter swaps size and strength for raw intelligence, giving Ben a form built for solving problems that brute force simply can't handle. His tiny stature lets him slip into places no other alien could reach.

Powers & Abilities

Grey Matter possesses an extremely high intellect, capable of understanding and operating complex technology almost instantly. His small size lets him access tight spaces, though it also leaves him physically vulnerable in a direct confrontation.

Background

Galvans are a highly advanced species known throughout the galaxy for their engineering and scientific achievements. Their small size has never been a limitation to their ambitions, and many of the era's most advanced inventions trace back to Galvan minds.
