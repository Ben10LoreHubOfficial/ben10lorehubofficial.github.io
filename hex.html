Villain

Hex

Human • Dark Sorcerer

Overview

Hex is a powerful and long-lived sorcerer who repeatedly clashes with Ben and his allies over various magical artifacts and dark rituals throughout the series.

Powers & Abilities

Hex commands a wide range of offensive and defensive spells, along with the ability to animate objects and summon mystical effects. His extensive magical knowledge, built up over centuries, makes him one of the more cerebral threats Ben faces.

Background

Little is known about Hex's true origins, though his age and mastery of magic suggest a history stretching back long before the series begins. He often seeks out ancient relics, believing they hold the key to even greater power.
