Villain

Vilgax

Chimera Sui Generis • Warlord of Vilgaxia

Overview

Vilgax stands as Ben Tennyson's most persistent and dangerous enemy, an intergalactic conqueror whose ambitions stretch far beyond Earth. His relentless pursuit of the Omnitrix has driven much of the series' overarching conflict.

Powers & Abilities

Vilgax possesses immense natural strength and durability, along with a variety of weapons and armor he's acquired over his long conquests. He is a skilled strategist and combatant, having fought and defeated countless enemies across the galaxy before ever crossing paths with Ben.

Background

Vilgax once ruled over his home world before turning his ambitions toward galactic conquest. His obsession with the Omnitrix stems from the immense power it represents — power he believes is rightfully his to claim and wield.
