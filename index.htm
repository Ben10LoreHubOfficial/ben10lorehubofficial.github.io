The Omnitrix

The Omnitrix is the watch-like alien device that lets Ben Tennyson transform into a wide range of extraterrestrial forms, each with unique powers and abilities.

  • Stores DNA samples from countless alien species across the universe
  • Has a built-in recalibration function that unlocks new random forms
  • Master Control mode removes the standard transformation time limit
  • Includes a self-destruct failsafe as a last-resort security measure
