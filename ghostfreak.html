Ghostfreak

Ectonurite • Phantom Alien

Overview

Ghostfreak is one of the more unsettling forms in Ben's collection, with a sinister design and an unnerving set of powers built around stealth and intimidation. There's more to this alien than meets the eye, with secrets tied to his species that go far beyond a simple transformation.

Powers & Abilities

Ghostfreak can turn invisible and intangible, allowing him to slip past enemies or through solid objects entirely. He also has retractable claws and a generally unsettling presence that can be used to unnerve opponents in a fight.

Background

Ectonurites are a mysterious species capable of phasing through matter, and their true forms are often hidden beneath an outer shell. Their reputation in the wider galaxy is mixed at best, with many other species treating them with deep suspicion.
