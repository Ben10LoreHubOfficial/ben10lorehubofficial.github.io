Villain

Dr. Animo

Human • Mutant Geneticist

Overview

Dr. Animo is a disgraced scientist who turned to twisted genetic experimentation after losing his medical license. His work focuses on transforming ordinary animals into monstrous, oversized mutates under his control.

Powers & Abilities

Animo himself isn't physically dangerous, but his transmutation technology lets him create powerful mutant creatures and command them in battle. He's resourceful and endlessly persistent, frequently returning with new and more dangerous creations.

Background

Once a respected doctor, Animo was stripped of his medical license after unethical experiments came to light. Rather than reform, he doubled down on his research, becoming one of Ben's most recurring and adaptable adversaries.
