Villain

Kevin 11

Osmosian • Energy Absorber

Overview

Kevin Levin's story is one of the more complex in the series, starting as a dangerous early antagonist before eventually becoming one of Ben's closest allies. His powers and motivations shift considerably as his character develops.

Powers & Abilities

As an Osmosian, Kevin can absorb the properties of materials he touches, temporarily taking on traits like the hardness of stone or the conductivity of metal. In his earliest appearances, he could absorb energy directly from electrical sources, which proved far more unstable and dangerous.

Background

Kevin's troubled childhood and uncontrolled powers pushed him toward villainy long before he met Ben. Over time, his arc shifts dramatically, exploring themes of redemption and control that set him apart from the show's other antagonists.
