Alien

Stinkfly

Lepidopterran • Aerial Scout

Overview

Stinkfly gives Ben the gift of flight along with a few unconventional offensive tricks. Equipped with four eyes on stalks and a pair of wings, he's a strange-looking but highly useful form for scouting and aerial combat.

Powers & Abilities

Stinkfly can fly at decent speed and maneuverability, and he can fire a sticky, slime-like substance from his abdomen to immobilize enemies. His eye stalks give him a wide field of view, making him great at spotting threats from a distance.

Background

Lepidopterrans are an insectoid species built for flight, with light bodies suited to life in the air. Their unusual appearance is often a source of jokes among other species, though their aerial abilities are nothing to underestimate.
