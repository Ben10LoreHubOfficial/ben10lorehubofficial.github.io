Villain

Charmcaster

Human • Sorceress

Overview

Charmcaster is a skilled young sorceress and the niece of the dark sorcerer Hex, often acting both alongside her uncle and independently in pursuit of magical power.

Powers & Abilities

Charmcaster wields a variety of magical spells and relies heavily on the Charms of Bezel, an enchanted bag capable of producing creatures and effects on command. Her magical skill makes her a consistently dangerous opponent despite lacking physical combat training.

Background

Trained by her uncle Hex from a young age, Charmcaster grew up steeped in dark magic and mystical tradition. Her loyalty occasionally wavers between her family's ambitions and her own personal goals, adding extra unpredictability to her character.
